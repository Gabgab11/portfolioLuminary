About

Ayomitunde Uji

Web Designer, Mobile App Developer & Cybersecurity Solutions. CEO, Luminary Digital Group.

Six years ago, I stumbled onto what would become Luminary Digital Group almost by accident: a small freelance build for a friend's restaurant that turned into a real appetite for making brands feel as good online as they do in person. What started as one project grew into a studio built around a simple idea: culture-first design that travels, backed by the technical range to build it properly, from the website itself to the mobile app and the security holding it all together.

Today, Luminary Digital Group works across America and Africa, partnering with hospitality, culture, and lifestyle brands who want their digital presence to carry the same weight as their story.
